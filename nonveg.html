Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

30 mins + marinating
Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, parsley, thyme, rosemary, garlic, salt, pepper, and honey.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over. Seal and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 8 hours.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grates.
  4. Remove chicken from the marinade and discard the excess. Grill for 6-8 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165¬∞F (74¬∞C).
  5. Transfer to a plate and let rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with lemon wedges and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

Shrimp Stir Fry

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
20 mins
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (slurry)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Green onions, sliced, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and cornstarch slurry. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add shrimp, season with salt and pepper, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until pink and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining oil. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, onion, and bell peppers for 3 minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Add broccoli and cook for another 3 minutes.
  5. Return shrimp to the skillet and pour the sauce over. Stir well and cook for 2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve hot over rice or noodles, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 300 | Protein: 28g | Carbs: 20g | Fat: 12g

Beef Bourguignon

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ
3 hrs
Hard

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups red wine (Burgundy preferred)
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish
  • Mashed potatoes or crusty bread, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. In a large Dutch oven, cook bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  3. Season beef with salt and pepper. Brown the beef in batches in the bacon fat and olive oil. Set aside.
  4. Add onions and garlic to the pot and saut√© for 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and carrots and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in tomato paste and flour. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  6. Pour in wine and broth, scraping up browned bits from the bottom. Add browned beef, bacon, thyme, and bay leaves.
  7. Bring to a simmer, cover, and transfer to the oven. Cook for 2-2.5 hours until beef is tender.
  8. Remove from oven, discard bay leaves, and season to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with parsley, alongside mashed potatoes or crusty bread.

Salmon with Dill Sauce

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ
30 mins
Medium

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped (plus more for garnish)
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon capers, drained (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
  • Steamed asparagus or roasted vegetables, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  3. Place salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet, skin side down.
  4. Drizzle salmon with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and chopped dill.
  5. Place lemon slices on top of each fillet.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  7. While salmon is baking, prepare the dill sauce by combining Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped dill, capers (if using), and honey in a bowl.
  8. Whisk until smooth and season with salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  9. Remove salmon from the oven and let rest for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Serve salmon topped with a generous spoonful of dill sauce, garnished with additional fresh dill and lemon wedges.
  11. Accompany with steamed asparagus or your favorite roasted vegetables.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 420 | Protein: 35g | Carbs: 8g | Fat: 27g

Turkey Meatballs

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ
45 mins
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground turkey (preferably not too lean)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (for cooking)
  • 3 cups marinara sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Additional grated Parmesan, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine breadcrumbs and milk. Let sit for 5 minutes to allow breadcrumbs to absorb the milk.
  2. Add ground turkey, Parmesan cheese, eggs, onion, garlic, parsley, oregano, basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper to the bowl.
  3. Mix gently with your hands until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, which can make the meatballs tough.
  4. Let the mixture rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. With damp hands, form the mixture into meatballs about 1.5 inches in diameter (about 2 tablespoons each).
  7. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  8. Working in batches, brown the meatballs on all sides, about 2 minutes per side. Transfer to the prepared baking sheet.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165¬∞F (74¬∞C).
  10. Meanwhile, warm marinara sauce in a large pot over medium-low heat.
  11. Once meatballs are done, transfer them to the pot with the marinara sauce and gently stir to coat.
  12. Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  13. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and additional grated Parmesan cheese.

Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ
8 hrs
Easy

Ingredients

  • 4-5 lbs pork shoulder (also called Boston butt)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons mustard powder
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 large onion, roughly chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce, plus more for serving
  • Burger buns or slider rolls, for serving
  • Coleslaw, for serving (optional)
  • Pickles, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, mustard powder, cumin, oregano, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to create a dry rub.
  2. Pat the pork shoulder dry with paper towels. Trim excess fat if desired, but leave some for flavor.
  3. Rub the pork shoulder all over with olive oil, then generously coat with the dry rub mixture, pressing it into the meat.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, sear the pork shoulder on all sides until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Place the chopped onion and minced garlic in the bottom of the slow cooker.
  6. Transfer the seared pork shoulder to the slow cooker, placing it on top of the onions and garlic.
  7. In a bowl, whisk together chicken broth, apple cider vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce. Pour this mixture around (not over) the pork in the slow cooker.
  8. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 5-6 hours, until the pork is very tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  9. Remove the pork from the slow cooker and transfer to a large cutting board or dish. Let rest for 10 minutes.
  10. Using two forks, shred the pork, discarding any large pieces of fat.
  11. Skim excess fat from the liquid in the slow cooker. If desired, strain the liquid to remove the cooked onions (or keep them for added flavor).
  12. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker. Add barbecue sauce and stir to combine with the cooking liquid.
  13. Cook on low for an additional 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  14. Serve hot on burger buns or slider rolls with additional barbecue sauce, coleslaw, and pickles if desired.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 450 | Protein: 38g | Carbs: 12g | Fat: 28g
