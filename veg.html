Creamy Mushroom Pasta

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
25 mins
Easy

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta (fettuccine or penne work well)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¬Ω cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and thyme, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, pour in heavy cream. Simmer for 3-4 minutes until it starts to thicken.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve garnished with fresh parsley and additional Parmesan if desired.

Spinach and Feta Stuffed Peppers

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
45 mins
Medium

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (red, yellow, or orange), halved and seeds removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 cups fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa (or rice)
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. Place halved peppers cut side up in the dish. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and bake for 10 minutes.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Saut√© onion for 3-4 minutes, then add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted. Remove from heat and add quinoa, feta, oregano, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  5. Spoon filling into peppers and top with breadcrumbs and Parmesan if using.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until peppers are tender and tops are golden.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
30 mins
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring water or broth to a boil. Add quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until water is absorbed.
  2. Fluff quinoa with a fork and let cool to room temperature.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olives, bell pepper, red onion, feta, and parsley.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add cooked quinoa to the vegetable mixture and drizzle with the dressing.
  6. Toss gently to combine. Adjust seasoning if necessary.
  7. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 300 | Protein: 8g | Carbs: 28g | Fat: 15g

Vegetable Lasagna

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
1 hr 15 mins
Medium

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Saut√© onion and garlic for 3 minutes.
  4. Add mushrooms, zucchini, and bell peppers. Cook for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  5. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Set aside.
  6. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  7. Spread 1 cup of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, followed by 1/3 of the vegetable mixture, 1 cup marinara, and 1 cup mozzarella. Repeat layers two more times.
  9. Finish with noodles, marinara sauce, and mozzarella on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Eggplant Parmesan

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
1 hr
Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 tablespoon salt (for sweating eggplants)
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil, for frying
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Arrange eggplant slices on a baking sheet and sprinkle with salt. Let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture. Pat dry with paper towels.
  3. In a shallow bowl, mix breadcrumbs, Parmesan, and Italian seasoning. Place flour in a separate bowl and beaten eggs in a third bowl.
  4. Dip each eggplant slice in flour, then egg, and finally breadcrumb mixture, pressing to coat well.
  5. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Fry eggplant slices until golden, about 2-3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer fried eggplant slices, marinara, mozzarella, and fresh basil. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella on top.
  7. Bake uncovered for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 450 | Protein: 18g | Carbs: 45g | Fat: 22g

Chickpea Curry

‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÖ‚òÜ
30 mins
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked basmati rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and saut√© until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in curry powder, cumin, and coriander. Cook for 1 minute to toast the spices.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and cook for 3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Stir in chickpeas, coconut milk, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.
  6. Stir in lemon juice just before serving.
  7. Serve hot over basmati rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
